How to verify somebody on Ferns

1

Enter your email



2

You’ll receive an email with a link.

Send the link to the person whose photo you want to verify.

3

The person who you have sent this link will send you a picture of themselves holding up numbers with their fingers via an email you’ll receive.

If the person is holding up the correct numbers, then feel safe to chat!

The email you receive will be through our own servers. The person you are communicating with doesn’t have access to your email.
