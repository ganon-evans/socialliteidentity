ReallyYou Identity Verification

Using your right hand, hold up the same number of fingers as the first number you see. For instance, if the number is two, hold up two fingers. If the number is zero, hold up your fist.

Using your left hand, hold up the same number of fingers as the second number you see. If you are unable to hold up your fingers, please hold up a written or digital version of the number you see.

Take a picture of yourself holding up your fingers either with the webcam of your computer or an uploaded picture. This picture will be sent to your friend, who will easily verify your identity!
